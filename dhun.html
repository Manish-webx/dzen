THE SPACE

For city dwellers to recover, rejuvenate and heal without leaving the city.

Nestled in the heart of Bandra on Mumbai’s bustling Linking Road, Dhun Wellness is a quiet revolution. Spanning over 6,000 sq. ft., it is a space designed for city-dwellers to heal, recover, and rejuvenate without leaving the city. At a time when urban life feels increasingly fragmented, Dhun Wellness is a return to wholeness.

WELLNESS PROGRAMS

Restore, Revitalise, Re-tune.

Designed for your unique needs, our 7-day programs combine Ayurvedic wisdom and modern therapies to help you rebalance and recharge.

Explore Programs
What does it include?

Each 7-day wellness program begins with a one-on-one consultation with our Wellness Director to understand your goals, followed by customised treatments, a tailored nutritional plan, and a take-home information docket to guide your journey. You’ll also have 24/7 access to our wellness concierge for continuous support throughout the program.

Is it customised to my specific needs

Yes, your program is fully customised to your needs - it is curated to address your primary goal while thoughtfully considering other concerns to optimise your time and results.

How do I prepare for it?

To prepare, simply schedule a one-on-one consultation with our Wellness Director, who will guide you through the next steps and design a program tailored to your needs.

THE EXPERTS

Meet the Founder

Mira Kapoor, the visionary behind Dhun Wellness, brings her deep passion for wellness and balance to life through this one-of-a-kind sanctuary.

Dhun Wellness is a reflection of her personal journey into self-discovery, conscious living, and a profound reverence for ancient wisdom.


Guided by her belief in the power of Ayurveda, sound, and intentional pause, Mira has created a space that brings ancient healing into the rhythm of modern life, rooted in tradition, shaped by science, and led with heart.
