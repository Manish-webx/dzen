Understanding Acne Scars Our Approach

Acne scarring is more than just a surface-level skin condition; it's often a reflection of internal imbalances.

At D'Zen Derma, we move beyond quick fixes and superficial treatments. Our approach is to identify the root cause—whether hormonal, dietary, or environmental—and create a comprehensive, customized protocol.

We utilize a blend of advanced clinical treatments and integrative wellness strategies to not only clear existing breakouts but also prevent future ones, ensuring long-term skin health and radiance.

Who Is This Treatment For?

01

Hormonal Breakouts

Deep, painful cysts predominantly located along the jawline, chin, and lower cheeks that fluctuate with your cycle.

02

Severe Boxcar Scars

Large, red, inflamed nodules deep beneath the skin surface that take weeks to heal and carry a high risk of permanent scarring.

03

Post-Acne Pigmentation

Residual damage from past breakouts, including indented (atrophic) scars, uneven texture, and dark hyperpigmentation spots.

04

Persistent Rolling Scars

Late-onset breakouts occurring well into your 20s, 30s, or 40s that require a delicate approach balancing acne scar treatment with anti-aging.

Q01 Are acne scar treatments painful?

Most of our acne scar treatments are minimally invasive and involve little to no discomfort. If clinical extractions or lasers are required, we ensure your comfort with numbing agents and careful techniques.

Q02 How long does it take to see results?

While some immediate reduction in inflammation can be seen after the first session, significant clearing typically takes 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the severity of the acne scars and the body's natural healing cycle.

Q03 What is the recovery time?

Recovery time is minimal. Chemical peels or micro-needling may result in a few days of mild redness or peeling. We will provide a strict aftercare routine to accelerate healing.

Q04 Will my acne scars come back?

Because we treat the root cause, long-term remission is highly likely. However, lifestyle changes, hormonal shifts, or stress can occasionally trigger minor breakouts, which can be managed with maintenance sessions.

Q05 Can all types of acne scars be completely removed?

While most acne scars can be significantly improved (by 70-90%), complete eradication depends on the scar's depth and type. Our multi-modality approach—such as combining subcision with advanced lasers—is designed to drastically smooth skin texture and yield permanent, highly noticeable improvements.

Q06 Is acne scar treatment safe for dark skin tones?

Absolutely. We carefully customize our protocols based on your specific skin type and tone. Advanced technologies like Microneedling Radiofrequency (MNRF) and specific clinical peels bypass the skin's surface layer, making them highly safe and effective for Indian skin tones without the risk of hyperpigmentation.

Q07 How many treatment sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies greatly depending on the severity, age, and type of your scarring. On average, most patients require between 3 to 6 sessions spaced a few weeks apart to achieve optimal results. We will provide a precise, personalized timeline during your initial consultation.
