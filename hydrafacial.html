The Treatment

What Is HydraFacial?

HydraFacial is a medical-grade hydradermabrasion treatment that combines cleansing, extraction, and hydration in a single, luxurious session. Using patented Vortex-Fusion technology, it delivers active ingredients deep into the skin while simultaneously extracting impurities.

Unlike traditional microdermabrasion, HydraFacial uses gentle water-based extraction—no crystals, no discomfort. The result is visibly clearer, more radiant skin with no downtime whatsoever.

"The treatment that celebrities swear by. Immediate glow, zero recovery time."

The Process

How It Works

01

Cleanse & Exfoliate

A gentle lactic acid peel loosens dead skin cells while the hydradermabrasion tip simultaneously clears away debris. Your skin emerges refreshed and prepped for deeper treatment.

02

Acid Peel

A specialized glycolic and salicylic acid blend deeply penetrates pores to loosen congestion and prepare skin for extraction—without the irritation of traditional peels.

03

Extract & Hydrate

The signature Vortex extraction nozzle painlessly removes debris from pores while infusing antioxidant-rich hyaluronic acid. Your skin drinks in deep hydration.

04

Protect & Rejuvenate

We finish with LED light therapy to reduce inflammation and stimulate collagen, followed by application of growth factor serums and SPF. Your skin glows instantly.

Your Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a HydraFacial take?

A standard HydraFacial takes approximately 45-60 minutes. At D'Zen Derma, our protocol includes additional steps like lymphatic drainage and LED therapy, bringing the total to 75-90 minutes for enhanced results.

Is there any downtime?

Zero downtime. You may experience mild redness for an hour or two post-treatment, but you can resume normal activities immediately. Many clients schedule HydraFacials during lunch breaks.

How often should I get HydraFacial?

For optimal results, we recommend monthly sessions to maintain skin health and build cumulative benefits. Some clients opt for bi-monthly treatments during seasons of high environmental stress.

Can I combine HydraFacial with other treatments?

Absolutely. HydraFacial pairs beautifully with treatments like Microcurrent, LED therapy, and certain laser treatments. We often recommend HydraFacial as a pre-event prep or as maintenance between more intensive procedures.

What skin type is HydraFacial best for?

HydraFacial is genuinely universal. From sensitive to acne-prone, dry to oily—our customized serums adapt to address your specific concerns. During your consultation, we'll select the perfect combination for your skin.

How soon will I see results?

Results are visible immediately. Your skin will look brighter, feel smoother, and have that coveted "glass skin" glow right after the session. These results typically last 4-6 weeks with proper maintenance.
